Computer & Network Security

Understanding threats, vulnerabilities, and how to stay safe online

Threats

Threats include malware like viruses, spyware and ransomware. Phishing and hacking are also common risks that can steal data.

Vulnerabilities

Weak passwords, outdated software, and unsecured networks create vulnerabilities that attackers can exploit.

Prevention

Strong passwords, antivirus software, updates, and avoiding suspicious links help protect systems.

Protection

Software should be updated regularly. Users must protect personal data and understand how their data is tracked online.
